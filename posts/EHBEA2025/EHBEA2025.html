EHBEA 2025 Conference Presentation

June 12, 2025 Conference

European Human Behaviour and Evolution Association

This year I had the pleasure of attending the European Human Behaviour and Evolution Association (EHBEA) conference in Newcastle, UK. I was also (coincidentally) part of the organising committee, which was a great experience in itself! The conference was a fantastic opportunity to present some work from my Master’s thesis, which investigated if, by changing certain facial features, we could make a face more or less attention grabbing. Null results throughout, apart from one significant difference between the attractiveness ratings of the average and distinctive versions of faces. It was my first ever conference presentation, and one I'll certainly remember. I was extremely nervous after finding out that there could be around 100 people in the audience. Overall it went well, and I had some positive feedback afterwards from people who attended, which was reassuring.
